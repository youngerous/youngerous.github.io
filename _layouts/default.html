Takyoung Kim

김탁영 (金卓映)

Hello! I am a Ph.D. Student in Conversational AI Lab at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign Computer Science, advised by Prof. Dilek Hakkani-Tür. Previously I received my Master's degree at Korea University, advised by Prof. Pilsung Kang.

My main research interest lies in solving problems related to human-machine communication in real-world settings. In particular, I am interested in building conversational agents that can interact beyond the textual domain, understand social nuances, and provide personalized assistance. I like to understand realistic constraints that current AI recipes struggle to handle.
